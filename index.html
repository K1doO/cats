ABOUT

The Feline Conservation Center is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) wild feline breeding facility by the Exotic Feline Breeding Compound (EFBC).

Natural habitat cages offer close-up viewing of some of the world`s rarest cats. We are located in Rosamond, California, just over an hour`s drive north of Los Angeles on Highway 14 (the Antelope Valley freeway). EFBC/FCC houses more than 70 wild cats ranging in size from the diminutive margay to larger species such as leopards, tigers, and jaguars.

Visit EFBC/FCC and learn more about some of nature`s most majestic creatures. Our Amur leopard, jaguar, Fishing Cat, and Pallas` Cat breeding programs are renowned worldwide.
